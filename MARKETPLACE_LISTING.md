```text
ABS Timecode Shifter is a professional utility designed for video editors, assistant editors, logging specialists, and transcriptionists who work with timecode logs in Google Docs.

Instead of manually recalculating and rewriting timecodes one-by-one, ABS Timecode Shifter automates the process with frame-accurate precision. Adjust entire columns of logs, transcripts, or notes in seconds.

### Key Features:
* **Frame-Accurate Calculations**: Supports standard frame rates: 23.976 fps, 24 fps, 25 fps, 29.97 fps (NDF & DF), 30 fps, 50 fps, 59.94 fps (NDF & DF), and 60 fps.
* **Flexible Scope Control**: Shift timecodes within your highlighted selection, from your cursor to the end of the document, or across the entire document.
* **Bi-directional Shifting**: Add or subtract time offset increments of hours, minutes, seconds, and frames.
* **Intelligent Search**: Automatically detects standard timecode formats (e.g., HH:MM:SS:FF or HH:MM:SS.FF) embedded within your document text.
* **Fast Processing**: Built with performance-optimized script search algorithms to process large documents in seconds.

### How to Use:
1. Open your Google Doc and launch the add-on from **Extensions > ABS Timecode Shifter > Start**.
2. Select your target frame rate (e.g., 23.976, 29.97 Drop Frame).
3. Set your search scope (e.g., Only Selection, From Cursor to End).
4. Enter the time offset you want to apply (e.g., +00:01:30:00).
5. Click **Shift Timecodes** and watch your document update instantly!

Save hours of manual calculations and avoid transcription errors with ABS Timecode Shifter.
```
