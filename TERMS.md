## 1. Description of Service
ABS Timecode Shifter is a utility add-on designed for Google Docs. It allows users to automatically find and shift timecodes (such as `01:23:45:12` or `01:23:45.12`) by a specified offset (hours, minutes, seconds, frames) across various frame rates.
