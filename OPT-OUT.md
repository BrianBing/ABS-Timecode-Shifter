# Draft Tester Opt-Out

If you are a draft tester for the **ABS Timecode Shifter** and wish to be removed from the tester list, please do one of the following:

1. **Open a GitHub Issue**: Go to our [GitHub Issues page](https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/abs-timecode-shifter/issues) and open an issue requesting to be removed.
2. **Contact the Developer**: Send an email request to the developer's support email address.

Upon receiving your request, we will remove your Google account from the draft tester list in the Google Cloud Console within 48 hours.
